        Shota Rustaveli (also known as Shota Rustaveli, Shota Rusthaveli, or Rusthaveli)
        was a medieval Georgian poet who lived in the 12th century. He is considered one
        of the greatest contributors to Georgian literature and is best known for his
        epic poem, "The Knight in the Panther's Skin" (also known as "The Knight in 
        the Panther Skin" or "Vepkhistqaosani" in Georgian).
